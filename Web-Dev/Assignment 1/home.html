Hello, and welcome to "The character database".
This site is designed to be a 'one stop' methodology for storing character information
This is in a "journal" as we'll call it here, where each character, item, spell, and weapon represents a "page". See the "About Site" for more.

General layout and how to use:

  • The below content is under heavy construction and not visible at the moment.
  • Create a new character, item, weapon or power from the dropdown "New Page...".
  • View your existing characters on the characters page.
  • View your items over on the items page.
  • View your powers, spells and abilities you have over on the abilities page.
  • And finally, see all your weapons over on the weapons page.

About the rest of the site

  • "Home" will bring you right back to this page.
  • The resources page has links to various off site character generators.
