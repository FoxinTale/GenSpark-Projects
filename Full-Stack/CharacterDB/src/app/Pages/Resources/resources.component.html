Personality tests. Before taking any of these, try and get a general idea of what you want this character to be like. Then, once you take one of these tests/quizzes, for every question ask yourself "Is this what this character would do?", "Is this how they would think or act?". Do your best to put yourself into the mind of this character and try to answer as them.
